About HILFS
Charity Organisation

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable communities through sustainable feeding, education, water, and health programs — rooted in dignity and faith.

  • 15+ water wells drilled
  • 8,000+ monthly meals
  • 6 schools built

Our Vision

A world where every child, family, and community has access to basic necessities, education, and the warmth of compassion — regardless of background.

  • Self‑sufficiency
  • Dignity for all

Objectives

  • Eliminate child hunger
  • Build places of worship & schools
  • Provide clean water
  • Distribute Zakat & Kurban effectively
  • Sponsor education
